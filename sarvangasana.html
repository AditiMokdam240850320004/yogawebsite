Sarvangasana (Shoulderstand)

The Queen of Asanas – Uplift your energy and body with balance and calm.

About Sarvangasana

Sarvangasana, or the Shoulderstand, is often called the "Queen of Asanas" due to its wide range of benefits. It involves balancing the body on the shoulders with legs extended upward and hands supporting the back.

This pose promotes mental clarity, stimulates the thyroid gland, and rejuvenates the nervous system, making it a powerful posture for full-body vitality.

Benefits of Sarvangasana

  • Stimulates the thyroid and parathyroid glands
  • Improves circulation and lymphatic drainage
  • Calms the nervous system and reduces stress
  • Enhances digestion and relieves constipation
  • Strengthens the back, shoulders, and legs
  • Aids in relieving varicose veins and hormonal imbalances
