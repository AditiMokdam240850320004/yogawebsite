Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose)

Awaken your spine and heart with this powerful backbend.

About Bhujangasana

Bhujangasana, or Cobra Pose, is a gentle backbend that helps open the chest and strengthen the spine. It is commonly included in warm-up sequences and sun salutations to activate the body's energy and improve posture.

This pose stretches the front body and increases flexibility in the back and shoulders while providing an uplifting mental effect.

Benefits of Bhujangasana

  • Strengthens the spine, shoulders, and arms
  • Opens the heart and lungs for better breathing
  • Relieves stress and mild depression
  • Improves flexibility in the lower back
  • Tones abdominal organs and aids digestion
  • Stimulates energy centers and reduces fatigue
