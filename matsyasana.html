Matsyasana (Fish Pose)

Open your heart and lungs with this powerful chest-expanding pose.

About Matsyasana

Matsyasana, or the Fish Pose, is a rejuvenating backbend that opens the chest, throat, and abdomen. Traditionally believed to destroy disease and stimulate energy flow, it's excellent for respiratory health and emotional release.

It is usually practiced as a counter-pose to shoulderstand and brings deep awareness to the breath and posture.

Benefits of Matsyasana

  • Opens the chest and improves breathing
  • Stimulates the thyroid and parathyroid glands
  • Relieves tension in the neck and shoulders
  • Improves spinal flexibility and posture
  • Aids in emotional release and heart chakra opening
  • Boosts energy and combats fatigue
