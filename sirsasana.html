Sirsasana (Headstand)

The King of Asanas – Transform your mind and body with this powerful pose.

About Sirsasana

Sirsasana, commonly known as the Headstand, is an advanced yoga pose that involves balancing the body inverted on the head and forearms. It is called the "King of Asanas" due to its numerous benefits for both body and mind.

This pose requires strength, balance, and focus and is often practiced to increase circulation, improve concentration, and calm the nervous system.

Benefits of Sirsasana

  • Improves blood circulation and oxygen flow to the brain
  • Strengthens shoulders, arms, and core muscles
  • Enhances concentration, mental clarity, and calmness
  • Helps relieve stress, anxiety, and mild depression
  • Stimulates the lymphatic system for detoxification
  • Improves digestion and metabolism
