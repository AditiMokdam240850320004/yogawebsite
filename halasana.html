Halasana (Plough Pose)

Stretch deep, calm the mind, and detoxify the body with this classic inversion.

About Halasana

Halasana, or the Plough Pose, is a calming inversion that stretches the spine, stimulates the abdominal organs, and brings mental clarity. The name "Hala" means plough in Sanskrit, symbolizing the tool that cultivates and prepares the earth—just as this pose prepares the body and mind for restoration and renewal.

Practicing Halasana regularly helps to improve flexibility, reduce fatigue, and restore balance to the nervous system.

Benefits of Halasana

  • Stretches the spine and shoulders deeply
  • Stimulates the thyroid and abdominal organs
  • Reduces stress, fatigue, and insomnia
  • Improves digestion and metabolism
  • Boosts blood circulation to the brain
  • Enhances flexibility and relaxation
